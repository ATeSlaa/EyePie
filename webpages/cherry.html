Scrumptious Apple Pie

Posted on Feburary 2nd, 2016

Kendra Curry

Cherry Pies are good. I'm not a huge fan of most cherry pies, however; this cherry pie is one of my alltime favorites. It's flakey crust, hot cherries and a scoop of ice cream will flip any day around. One of my favorite cookbooks is My Italian Kitchen.

Prep time: 15 min

Cook time: 55 min

Total time: 70 min

Serving size: 8

Calories per serving: 160

Ingredients:

Ingredients for the Filling

  • 4 cups cherries, pitted
  • 1 cup sugar 1 1/4 cup for sour cherries
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 9in pastry for a douple-crust

Directions:

  1. 1. Mix ingredients for filling.
  2. 2. Place in pastry-lined pie pan
  3. 3. Put top crust on. Cut vent slits in top crust, and flute edges.
  4. 4. Brush with milk and sprinkle with sugar.
  5. 5. Bake at 425 F for 25 minutes, reduce heat to 350F and bake for 25-30 minutes more.
  6. Tip: put sugar, flour and cinnamon in a large zip-loc bag. Add the cherries and toss to coat. This coats them more even than doing it in a bowl and the filling is firmer.
