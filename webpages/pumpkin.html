Perfect Pumpkin Pie

Posted on March 3rd, 2016

Jenni Smith

The Perfect Pumpkin Pie is just that. This pumpkin pie is a perfect ending to a Thanksgiving feast. The great thing about Pumpkin Pie is that you can add ice cream and a cup of coffee to end a great day! I love using Betty Crocker's Cookbook

Prep time: 15 min

Cook time: 55 min

Total time: 70 min

Serving size: 8

Calories per serving: 379

Ingredients:

Ingredients for the Filling

  • 1 15 ounce can pumpkin
  • 1 14 can Sweetended Condense Milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp ground ginger
  • 1/2 tsp ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 9 inch unbaked pie crust

Directions:

  1. 1. Preheat oven to 425 degree F. Whisk pumpkin, sweetened condensed milk, eggs, spices and salt in medium bowl until smooth. Pour into crust. Bake 15 minutes.
  2. 2. Reduce oven temperature to 350 degrees F and continue baking 35 to 40 minutes or until knife inserted 1 inch from crust comes out clean. Cool. Garnish as desired. Store leftovers covered in refrigerator.
