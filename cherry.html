Scrumptious Apple Pie

Posted on Feburary 2nd, 2016

Kendra Curry

Cherry Pies are good. I'm not a huge fan of most cherry pies, however; this cherry pie is one of my alltime favorites. It's flakey crust, hot cherries and a scoop of ice cream will flip any day around. One of my favorite cookbooks is My Italian Kitchen.

Prep time: 15 min

Cook time: 55 min

Total time: 70 min

Serving size: 8

Calories per serving: 160

Ingredients:

Ingredients for the Filling

  • 4 cups cherries, pitted
  • 1 cup sugar 1 1/4 cup for sour cherries
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 9in pastry for a douple-crust

Directions:

  • Mix ingredients for filling.
  • Place in pastry-lined pie pan
  • Put top crust on. Cut vent slits in top crust, and flute edges.
  • Brush with milk and sprinkle with sugar.
  • Bake at 425 F for 25 minutes, reduce heat to 350F and bake for 25-30 minutes more.
  • Tip: put sugar, flour and cinnamon in a large zip-loc bag. Add the cherries and toss to coat. This coats them more even than doing it in a bowl and the filling is firmer.
